## Key Thresholds

**Core Web Vitals (Good):**
- LCP: 2.5s or less
- INP: 200ms or less
- CLS: 0.1 or less

**Performance Budgets:**
- Total page weight: < 1.5 MB
- JavaScript: < 300 KB
- CSS: < 100 KB

**Lighthouse Targets:**
- Performance: 90+
- Accessibility: 100
- Best Practices: 95+
- SEO: 95+
